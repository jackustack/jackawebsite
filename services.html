What's Included

The Problem

Most companies have tags firing that nobody fully understands. GTM containers accumulate cruft. GA4 events are misconfigured. Conversion pixels double-fire or don't fire at all. And when this data reaches your warehouse, nobody trusts it enough to make decisions.

What I Do

I audit every tag on your site — GTM, GA4, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight, whatever you're running — and map it to its downstream destination. I identify what's broken, what's redundant, and what's missing. Then I fix the entire chain so clean data flows from your site instrumentation through to your reporting layer.

Deliverables

Tag inventory and audit report, remediation implementation, tag governance documentation, and QA validation confirming data accuracy from pixel to warehouse. Typical engagement: 2–4 weeks.

Who This Is For

Marketing teams that don't trust their analytics data. Revenue ops teams reconciling mismatched conversion numbers. Any company where "the numbers don't match" has become a recurring meeting topic.

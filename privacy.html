Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Overview

Jack Tyler ("I", "me") operates jackatyler.com. This page explains what data is collected when you visit this website and how it is handled.

Data Collection

This website is a static site. At launch, it does not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics services, or third-party resources. No personal data is collected through the website itself.

If you contact me via email, I will receive your email address and any information you choose to include. I use this information only to respond to your inquiry.

Hosting

This site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server log data (IP address, browser type, request timestamps) as part of normal web server operations. See Vercel's Privacy Policy (opens in new tab) for details.

Cookies

This website does not set any cookies. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and a cookie consent mechanism will be implemented before any cookies are set.

Third-Party Services

This website does not load any third-party scripts, fonts, or tracking pixels. All assets are self-hosted.

Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data I hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your personal data
  • Data portability

Lawful basis: Where personal data is processed (e.g., responding to emails), the lawful basis is legitimate interest (responding to your direct inquiry).

Your Rights Under CPRA (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information: I do not sell or share personal information as defined under the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

Data Retention

I do not store personal data collected through this website beyond standard server logs maintained by Vercel. Email correspondence is retained only as long as needed to address your inquiry.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date. Material changes (such as adding analytics or cookies) will be clearly noted.

Contact

For privacy-related requests or questions, contact me at jack@jackatyler.com.
